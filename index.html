Chapter VII.

Questions before you decide.

Is this simply ChatGPT with a skin on top?

No. OpenClaw runs on local large language models (Ollama) on your own computer by default — zero cost per run. It only escalates to Claude or GPT for specific tasks that need premium reasoning, and you set a hard daily dollar cap. Most A.I. marketing tools bill per token with no cap. We bill flat per month.

Will it replace a human marketer?

If you currently have no marketer and cannot afford one — yes, it replaces that gap. If you have a $5K-a-month agency — it does not replace the strategic level, but it does replace sixty to seventy per cent of the execution. Many Pro customers use it to retire their agency and keep a strategist on retainer.

Do I need to be technical?

Starter tier: yes, enough to follow a twenty-minute install walkthrough. Pro tier: no — we install it for you on a Zoom call. If you can use Gmail and read a recipe, you can handle Pro.

What hardware do I need?

Minimum sixteen gigabytes of memory and a discrete graphics card with eight gigabytes of VRAM. Recommended is thirty-two gigabytes of memory and twelve-plus gigabytes VRAM (an RTX 4070 or better, or an M2 Pro Mac). No GPU? You can still run with cloud escalation covering heavy tasks — budget roughly $30 a month of Claude spend.

What if my website is hot garbage?

Either we add content and make it work (Stack Migration, included in Pro), or we rebuild it on Webflow in fourteen days (Site Rebuild add-on, $3,500). Either way, you own the final result. We never host your site.

How is this different from Lindy, Relevance A.I., or Gumloop?

Those are cloud-first horizontal tools priced per token — lovely for tech-forward startups, painful for contractors. OpenClaw runs locally, caps cloud spend, and is built for one specific kind of buyer with pre-wired workflows for marketing — not build-your-own-agent frameworks.

What happens to my data?

It stays on your computer. Local models read and process everything on your machine. Your brand voice samples, your email contents, your client lists — none of it leaves. The only data we store is your billing information and your waitlist email.

Is there a free trial?

A thirty-day money-back, no-questions-asked refund. Or use the self-host open-source version forever at zero dollars (community support only). You are never forced to pay to try it.

When does it ship?

The private beta is running now with Golden Maple Landscaping in Barrie, Ontario. Public beta opens June 2026 in batches of ten. Ontario contractors and regulated small businesses are prioritised.

Can I see the code?

Yes — the core is MIT-licensed and will be on GitHub before public beta. Self-host it, fork it, audit it. The commercial tiers fund the roadmap, hosted updates, and support.
