· FAQ ·

Questions before you decide.

Is this just ChatGPT with a skin on it?

No. OpenClaw runs on local LLMs (Ollama) on your computer by default — zero cost per run. Only escalates to Claude or GPT for specific tasks that need premium reasoning, and you set a hard daily dollar cap. Most AI marketing tools bill per token with no cap. We bill flat per month.

Will it replace a human marketer?

If you currently have no marketer and can't afford one — yes, it replaces that gap. If you have a $5K/mo agency — it probably doesn't replace them at the strategic level, but it replaces 60-70% of the execution. Many Pro customers use it to fire their agency and keep a strategist on retainer.

Do I need to be technical?

Starter tier: yes, enough to follow a 20-minute install walkthrough. Pro tier: no — we install it for you on a Zoom call. If you can use Gmail and read a recipe, you can handle Pro.

What hardware do I need?

Minimum: 16 GB RAM and a discrete GPU with 8+ GB VRAM. Recommended: 32 GB RAM and 12+ GB VRAM (RTX 4070 or better, or an M2 Pro Mac). No GPU? You can still run with cloud escalation covering heavy tasks — budget ~$30/mo of Claude/GPT spend.

What if my website is hot garbage?

Either we add content to it and make it work (Stack Migration, included in Pro), or we rebuild it on Webflow in 14 days (Site Rebuild add-on, $3,500). Either way, you own the final result. We never host your site.

How is this different from Lindy, Relevance AI, Gumloop?

Those are cloud-first horizontal tools priced per token — great for tech-forward startups, painful for contractors. OpenClaw runs locally, caps cloud spend, and is built for one specific kind of buyer (contractors, clinics, solo operators) with pre-wired workflows for marketing — not "build-your-own-agent" frameworks.

What happens to my data?

It stays on your computer. Local LLMs read and process everything on your machine. Your brand voice samples, your email contents, your client lists — never leave. The only data we store is your Stripe billing info and your waitlist email.

Is there a free trial?

30-day money-back, no-questions-asked refund. Or use the self-host OSS version forever at $0 (community support only). You're never forced to pay to try it.

When does it ship?

Private beta is running now with Golden Maple Landscaping (Barrie, ON). Public beta opens June 2026 in batches of 10. Join the waitlist to get an install invite — regulated SMBs (legal, healthcare, accounting) and Ontario contractors are prioritized.

Can I see the code?

Yes — the core is MIT-licensed and will be on GitHub before public beta. Self-host it, fork it, audit it. The commercial tiers fund the roadmap, hosted updates, and support.
