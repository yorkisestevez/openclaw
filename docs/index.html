Start here.

OpenClaw is a local-first AI marketing-ops toolkit. 13+ specialized agents — copywriter, social manager, review responder, invoice collector, and more — running on your own computer and publishing through your existing stack.

These docs walk you through installing OpenClaw on your own hardware, connecting your Google / Meta / CMS / email accounts, and running your first autonomous marketing cycle.

New here? The fastest path: check requirements → install on Windows (or Mac) → connect Google. About 20 minutes end to end.

What you're building

By the end of setup you'll have:

  • A Command Deck at http://localhost:5055 showing every agent in your party, their XP, and the live queue
  • 4 read-only agents running autonomously: inbox triage, follow-up tracking, meeting prep, competitive intel
  • 9 publishing agents that wait for your ✅ before sending — Gmail drafts, FB/IG posts, calendar events, review replies, invoice chasers
  • A per-client voice sample library that compounds every time you approve a draft

How it runs

OpenClaw is a local Node.js process with a SQLite queue and a Command Deck dashboard. Your local LLM (Ollama with qwen / deepseek / gemma) does 90% of the heavy lifting at zero per-run cost. Anything that needs premium reasoning — complex legal drafting, long-document synthesis — escalates to Claude at a hard-capped daily spend you set.

Your content, email, client lists, voice samples — never leave your machine. Only the specific prompt that escalates to Claude leaves, and we show you exactly what's being sent.
